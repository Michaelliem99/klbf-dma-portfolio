Outlet Coverage

Outlet Coverage is the number of outlets getting covered by a business within his quota of outlets to be covered in a specified time frame.

There are number of reasons why outlet coverage is an important process for businesses:

  • Controlling and increase salesman effectiveness.
  • Help management to keep track their achievement, which may improve the consistency and reliability of sales.
  • Shorten operational duration.

Benefit & Value :

  • More accurate demand forecast based on proven statistical method
  • Reduce stock-out and overstocking
  • Minimize lost business due to out of stock

Our Focus :

  • How much is the achievement between the target and actual.

Outlet Segmentation

Outlet Segmentation is the process by which we divided our customers (outlets) up based on common characteristics.

There are number of reasons why outlet segmentation is an important, including:

  • Makes marketing plans easier.
  • Helps company to focus on certain outlets group instead of targeting mass market.
  • Allows company to learn more about customers.

Benefit & Value :

  • Retention of customer
  • Wise and efficient use of resources

Our Focus :

  • Recency: The freshness of the outlet purchases
  • Frequency: The frequency of the outlet transactions
  • Monetary: The purchasing power of outlet

Product Correlation

Product correlation is the measurement of how far two products move in relation to one another.

Product correlation is an important measurement for businesses for a variety of reasons which includes:

  • Understanding the dynamics of product relationships allows businesses to make more informed and effective decisions.
  • Adjusts sales strategy based on product relationship.
  • Reveal interdependencies between products.

Benefit & Value :

  • Maximize customer engagement.
  • Develop cross-selling strategies.

Our Focus :

  • Product placement: increased sales are made possible by more effective product placement based on their relationship.

Product Relationship (Product Bundling)

Product relationship is the process of grouping together several products based on their complimentary relationship.

Product relationships are important for businesses for a variety of reasons, including:

  • Assist consumers in reducing decision-making stress.
  • Assist businesses in increasing sales and profits.
  • Give customers more value for a single purchase than they would if they bought individual products.

Benefit & Value :

  • Persuade customers to purchase more products.
  • Reduces marketing and distribution expenses.
  • Increase inventory clearance.

Our Focus :

  • Customer purchase pattern: Combine related products based on customer buying habits to increase product appeal.

  • Introduce customer to enticing products they haven't considered

Our Focus :

Route Optimization

Route optimization provides salesman with the best hospitals to visit for their biweekly visitation plan and its optimal visit route based on shortest route concept.

There are number of reasons why route optimization system is an important process for businesses :

  • Route optimization helps sales manager in creating a visit plan for salesman from dozens of hospitals to choose from based on specific criteria and survey results.

Benefit & Value :

  • Automation of visit plan creation based on specific criteria and survey results every 2 weeks.

Our Focus :

  • Outlet Choosing Accuracy: better accuracy in choosing which outlet to visit will increase the salesman performance.
  • Shortest Route: shorter route helps salesman in having a more efficient travel time between each outlet.

Route Optimization

Sales and demand forecasting refers to making estimations about future customer demand using historical data and other information.

There are number of reasons why route optimization system is an important process for businesses :

  • It allows businesses to more effectively optimize inventory, increasing turnover rates and reducing holding costs.
  • It provides an insight into upcoming cash flow, meaning businesses can more accurately budget to pay suppliers and other operational costs.
  • Anticipating demand means knowing when to increase staff and other resources to keep operations running smoothly during peak periods.

Without demand forecasting, businesses risk making poor decisions about their products and target markets.

Benefit & Value :

  • More accurate demand forecast based on proven statistical method
  • Reduce stock-out and overstocking
  • Minimize lost business due to out of stock

Our Focus :

  • Forecast Accuracy: better forecast accuracy gives better support to company in taking decision of production, distribution, and supply planning.
